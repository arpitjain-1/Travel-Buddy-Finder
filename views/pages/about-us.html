About Travel Buddy Finder

India's premier platform for connecting travelers

Our Story

Travel Buddy Finder was born from a simple idea: exploring India is better when shared with the right companion. Founded in 2023, we've been on a mission to connect like-minded travelers and foster meaningful connections throughout the diverse landscapes of India.

What started as a small community of adventure seekers in Mumbai has grown into a nationwide network of travelers helping each other discover the incredible beauty and culture that India has to offer.

Our Mission

To create a safe, inclusive platform where Indian travelers can find compatible companions, share experiences, and make the most of their journeys across our beautiful country.

Our Values

Safety First

We verify profiles and provide tools for secure communication between members across India.

Community

We foster a supportive community of Indian travelers who help each other explore and grow.

Inclusivity

We welcome travelers from all backgrounds, cultures, and regions of India.

Innovation

We constantly improve our platform to better serve our Indian community's needs.

Exploring India Together

India is a vast country with incredible diversity - from the snow-capped Himalayas to the tropical beaches of Kerala, from the deserts of Rajasthan to the lush greenery of the Northeast. With such variety, finding the right travel companion who shares your interests and travel style can transform your journey.

East & Northeast
