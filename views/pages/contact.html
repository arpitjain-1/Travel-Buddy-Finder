Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I find a travel buddy?

Finding a travel buddy is easy with our platform. Simply create an account, set up your profile with your travel preferences, search for travelers going to your desired destination, and connect with them through our messaging system. You can also post your travel plans and let potential buddies find you!

Is it safe to travel with someone I met online?

Safety is our top priority. We have a verification system in place to ensure users are who they claim to be. We also encourage you to chat extensively with potential travel buddies before meeting, start with a public meetup, and always trust your instincts. Additionally, we provide safety guidelines and tips for traveling with new companions.

Do I need to pay to use Travel Buddy Finder?

Travel Buddy Finder offers both free and premium membership options. With a free account, you can create a profile, search for travel companions, and receive messages. Premium members get additional benefits like advanced search filters, unlimited messaging, profile highlighting, and access to exclusive events and meetups.

How do I cancel or modify my travel plans?

You can easily update your travel plans through your profile dashboard. If you've already connected with a travel buddy, it's considerate to inform them as soon as possible about any changes. You can use our messaging system to communicate these changes or cancel plans if necessary.

What destinations are covered by Travel Buddy Finder?

Travel Buddy Finder focuses on Indian destinations, covering all major tourist spots like Goa, Kerala, Rajasthan, Himachal Pradesh, Ladakh, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Northeast India, and many more. We're continually expanding our database to include emerging travel hotspots across India.
